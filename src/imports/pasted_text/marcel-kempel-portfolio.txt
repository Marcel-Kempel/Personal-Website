Create an award-winning personal portfolio website design in Figma for Marcel Kempel.

This is NOT a company website and NOT an agency landing page. It is a personal portfolio and CV website for a Business Informatics student in Germany who is building a strong profile around process automation, digitalization, data analysis, and practical IT implementation.

The website should feel premium, calm, structured, analytical, and trustworthy. It should make recruiters, professors, thesis supervisors, and future business contacts think: “This person is young, but very structured, technically capable, practical, and serious.”

Use professional German copy throughout the visible UI.

Main goal of the website:
Present Marcel Kempel as a strong Wirtschaftsinformatiker with a clear focus on:

* Prozessautomatisierung
* Digitalisierung
* Datenanalyse
* Reporting
* Enterprise Architecture
* praktische IT-Umsetzung
* Prozessverständnis zwischen Business und IT

Do not make the page feel like a freelancer agency website. It should be personal first, but already show a future direction toward consulting and implementation.
